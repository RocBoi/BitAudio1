RocBoi Quez

Genre: Hip-Hop / Trap / Conscious Rap

RocBoi Quez is an Atlanta-born rapper and composer delivering gritty lyricism, unfiltered truth, and magnetic sound. With roots deep in the South’s hip-hop legacy and a futuristic mindset, RocBoi Quez blends cosmic vision with street realism. As the first official BitAudioX artist, he is pioneering blockchain-based music monetization and decentralized fan connection.

His latest single, “Summer of The Real” ft. Wiz Khalifa, distributed through Bentley Records, sets the tone for a new era of hip-hop elevation—raw, reflective, and ready for the global stage.
